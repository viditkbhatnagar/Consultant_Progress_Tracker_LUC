## Section C — Information you are giving consent for

I confirm that I have read and understood:

- The [Privacy Policy](01-privacy-policy.md), and
- The [Children's Privacy Notice](07-childrens-privacy-notice.md).

I give my consent for `[FILL: Skillhub Training / Institute, division of
registered company name]` to collect, store, and process personal data
about my child as described in those documents, including:

- Identity, contact, academic, and financial data.
- Free-text notes by counselors about meetings and follow-ups with my
  child or with me.
- Storage of this data on MongoDB Atlas in Ireland and processing
  through Render in Singapore — i.e., a four-jurisdiction footprint
  (UAE → Singapore → Ireland → optionally the United States for the
  AI features used by staff in adult contexts).

## Section E — Your rights

I understand that I retain the right to:

- See the data Skillhub holds about my child.
- Correct inaccurate data.
- Withdraw any of the above consents at any time, free of charge, by
  emailing `[FILL: privacy@…]`.
- Ask Skillhub to delete data when no longer needed and no other
  lawful basis applies.
- Lodge a complaint with the UAE Data Office or with the EU lead
  supervisory authority `[FILL]`.

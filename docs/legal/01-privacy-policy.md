under the same lawful basis as the rest of the field but we may ask you
to remove it.

## 5. Where your data is stored and processed

We host the Platform with the following sub-processors:

| Hop | Vendor | Region |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **MongoDB Atlas** (primary database) | Ireland (EU/EEA) |
| 2 | **Render** (application hosting) | Singapore |
| 3 | **OpenAI** (LLM — analysis, embeddings, fallback) | United States |
| 4 | **Groq** (primary LLM for in-app document chat) | United States |

A typical request from a UAE user therefore touches four jurisdictions
(UAE → Singapore → Ireland → optionally the United States). Full vendor
detail and data-flow narrative is in the
[Sub-processor List](06-subprocessor-list.md).

The Platform does **not** use cookies. It does store your authentication

## 11. Security

We protect your data with technical and organisational measures
described in our [Information Security Policy](../security/01-information-security-policy.md).
These include access control, role-based scoping, encryption in transit
to all sub-processors, encryption at rest at MongoDB Atlas, password
hashing with bcrypt, and an [Incident Response Plan](../security/07-incident-response-plan.md)
that obliges us to notify regulators within 72 hours of becoming aware of
a notifiable breach.

We are honest about gaps in our current state — see our
[Security Gap Roadmap](../security/14-security-gap-analysis-and-remediation-roadmap.md).
This roadmap is for our internal teams; you can ask the DPO for a
high-level summary of items that may affect you.

## 12. Children

The Platform is used to manage records of minors enrolled in our
Skillhub Training and Skillhub Institute branches. We process minors'
data only on the lawful basis of parental/guardian consent and only to
the extent necessary for managing the educational service.

Read the [Children's Privacy Notice](07-childrens-privacy-notice.md).
A [Parental Consent Form](08-parental-consent-form.md) is collected at
admission.

## 13. AI features — what we do and don't do

The Platform uses third-party AI models (Groq's Llama 3.3 70B as primary
and OpenAI as fallback / for embeddings) to answer staff questions about
LUC programmes and to generate analytical summaries.

- The **Documents-RAG chat** is available to LUC staff only.
- Tool results returned to the AI may include personal data including, in
  the case of the tracker chatbot, names and contact details of leads
  and students.
- Until our DPAs with these vendors are confirmed and additional
  redaction is in place (see
  [Security Gap Roadmap](../security/14-security-gap-analysis-and-remediation-roadmap.md),
  SEC-05 / SEC-13), we do **not** invite parents or students to use the
  AI features directly.
- We do **not** use your data to train the AI models. Per the relevant
  vendor API terms, content sent to the API is not used to improve their
  base models. We will update this paragraph once the DPAs are signed.

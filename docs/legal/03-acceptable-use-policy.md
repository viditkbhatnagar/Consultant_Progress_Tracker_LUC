# Acceptable Use Policy

> v0.1 — drafted 2026-04-26 · Effective date: `[FILL]` · Last reviewed: 2026-04-26 · Owner: Security lead `[FILL]`

This Policy describes how staff may and may not use the Team Progress
Tracker. It binds anyone with credentials to the Platform.

## You may

- Use the Platform for the legitimate business purposes assigned to your
  role.
- Export data through the Export Center within your role's allowance,
  for legitimate reporting needs.
- Ask the in-app chat questions about LUC programmes (LUC users only)
  and about your own org's data.
- Report bugs, suggestions, and security concerns to the engineering and
  security leads.

## You must not

- Share your password or access token with anyone, including colleagues.
- Use a colleague's account, even with their permission.
- Export, screenshot, or transmit personal data outside Learners
  Education's approved tools without DPO authorisation.
- Paste personal data — particularly minors' data — into any external
  AI tool, search engine, or third-party service that does not have a
  written processing agreement with us.
- Attempt to bypass the role-based scoping in the Platform (for example,
  by manipulating URLs, request bodies, or query strings to access data
  outside your assigned organisation or team).
- Use the Platform to harass, defame, or send unsolicited commercial
  communications.
- Run scripts or automation against the Platform without prior approval
  from the engineering lead.
- Attempt to reverse-engineer, decompile, or extract embeddings,
  prompts, or model weights from the AI features.
- Upload malware, conduct security testing without prior written
  authorisation, or attempt to deny service.
- Edit or fabricate audit fields (`createdBy`, `lastUpdatedBy`).

## Specific rules for AI features

- AI outputs are not authoritative. Verify before relying on them.
- Do not paste minors' personal data into the chat drawer until the DPO
  confirms the AI vendor DPAs are in place (see
  [Security Gap Roadmap](../security/14-security-gap-analysis-and-remediation-roadmap.md)
  SEC-05 / SEC-13).
- Do not use the AI features to generate fictitious leads, fabricate
  meeting notes, or otherwise mislead the audit trail.

## Specific rules for the Export Center

- Exports may contain personal data. Treat downloaded files at the
  highest classification of any field they contain.
- Do not store exports on personal devices or personal cloud accounts.
- Delete export files from your local machine when no longer needed.
- The pivot endpoint is rate-limited (5 requests / minute / user). Do
  not attempt to circumvent the limit.

## Reporting

If you witness a violation, or believe your account has been
compromised, contact the security lead `[FILL: security@…]` immediately.
We will treat the report confidentially.

## Enforcement

Wilful violation may lead to:

- Immediate account suspension.
- Disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment or
  contract.
- Referral to law enforcement.
- Civil action.
